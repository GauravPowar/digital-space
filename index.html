// The Daily Brew Ritual
Weekdays
Espresso → Dedica Duo
Mornings move fast. The Dedica Duo is on before anything else — grind, tamp, pull. 12–13g in, watching the shot drop into a cold cup. No time for a slow brew. Just get it right and get going.
Weekends
Pour Over → Hario V60
No rush. Kettle on. Bloom the grounds. Spiral pours, counting seconds between each. The V60 rewards patience — a quiet Saturday morning ritual that earns its cup. Qualifying on in the background.
Race Sunday
Extra V60. Always.
Race day is still a weekend, so the V60 stays. Two cups before lights out. The slower the brew, the more deliberate the race watching. Grids forming on screen, bloom settling in the dripper.
The Log
Every extraction documented.
Whether espresso or pour over — dose, yield, and taste notes all go into the logbook at coffeeforgaurav.pages.dev. The data obsession never stops, even on rest days.
